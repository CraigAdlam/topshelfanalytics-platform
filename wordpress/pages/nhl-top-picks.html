NHL player shot prediction picks based on Top Shelf Analytics’ 2+ shots model. This public preview focuses on historical and upcoming 2+ shot predictions, model performance metrics, shot distribution indicators, and final hit/miss results.
Table Notes

Each row represents one player prediction for 2+ shots on a given prediction date.

Prediction probability and model performance metrics come from NEXTGAME_master.csv. Actual results are updated using the shots column from skater_summary.csv.

Distribution metrics include mean, median, mode, skew, and concentration. Concentration measures the share of prior games where a player’s shot total finished within ±1 of their median shot total as of the model cutoff date.

Pending rows indicate games where the prediction exists, but final shot results have not yet been joined.
